Spicy drunken noodles recipes

Disciption

This is a spicy noodles recipes

Ingridients

  • 6 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 6 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil, or more as needed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bulb shallot, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced, or more to taste
  • 1 pound boneless chicken, thinly sliced
  • 1 large green bell pepper, sliced into strips
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 sprigs fresh basil, leaves removed and torn

Steps

  1. Whisk oyster sauce, white sugar, vinegar, and lime juice together in a bowl until sauce is smooth.
  2. Pour enough oil into a wok or large skillet to coat the bottom and turn to medium-high heat. Cook and stir onion, shallot, and garlic in hot oil until fragrant and slightly softened, 2 to 3 minutes. Add chicken and bell pepper; cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink, 5 to 7 minutes. Move chicken mixture to 1 side of wok.
  3. Place rice noodles in a bowl and fill with hot water, separating the noodles with a fork or your hands; soak until softened, about 45 minutes.
  4. Pour egg into the hot wok; cook until it becomes slightly set, 1 to 2 minutes. Flip egg and cook until set, 1 to 2 minutes more. Break egg apart with a fork and stir into chicken mixture.
  5. Mix basil and sauce into chicken mixture. Drain noodles and add to chicken mixture; cook and stir until heated through, 2 to 3 minutes.
